We welcome your participation in the CTI: FTAAP Dialogue on Trade and Labour, which will bring together APEC trade officials, government representatives, international organizations, business associations, and invited experts to exchange perspectives on labour provisions in regional and free trade agreements.

APEC member economies are invited to nominate self-funded participants for the dialogue by August 10, 2026 (Singapore Standard Time). As the dialogue will be held on the margins of the Third APEC Senior Officials' Meeting (SOM3), participants attending through an APEC economy delegation must also complete official SOM3 accreditation through their economy's Delegation Accreditation Officer (DAO).

Participants not attending through an APEC economy delegation should work directly with the Project Overseers regarding accreditation. Failure to complete the required SOM3 registration process will result in participants being unable to attend the dialogue.

For additional information regarding participant nominations, accreditation, dialogue logistics, agenda, or technical content, please contact the appropriate point of contact below.
