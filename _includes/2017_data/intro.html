About

The CTI FTAAP Dialogue on Trade and Labour will bring together APEC trade officials, government representatives, international organizations, business associations, and invited experts to examine labour-related provisions in regional and free trade agreements across the Asia-Pacific. Through expert presentations and economy exchanges, participants will explore common approaches, implementation experiences, and the role of technical assistance, capacity building, and stakeholder engagement in supporting labour provisions in trade agreements. The dialogue will be informed by key findings from the APEC Policy Support Unit's study on convergences and divergences in labour provisions across trade agreements in the region.
