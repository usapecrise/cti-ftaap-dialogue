CTI: FTAAP Dialogue on Trade and Labor

August 24, 2026 · Dalian, China

APEC member economies are invited to nominate self-funded participants for the CTI: FTAAP Dialogue on Trade and Labor. The dialogue will bring together APEC trade officials, government representatives, international organizations, business associations, and invited experts to exchange perspectives on labor provisions in regional and free trade agreements.

Please submit your participant details using the sign up form provided.

Participant Note

Please submit participant nominations by August 10, 2026 (Singapore Standard Time). Participants attending through an APEC economy delegation must also complete official SOM3 registration through their economy's Delegation Accreditation Officer (DAO). Participants not attending through an APEC economy delegation should work with the Project Overseers regarding accreditation. Failure to complete the required SOM3 registration process will result in participants being unable to attend the dialogue.
